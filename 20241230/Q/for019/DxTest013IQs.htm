Legal and Ethical Considerations in Digital Forensics Quiz

1. What is the main legal issue in digital forensics?
Admissibility of Evidence
2. Which law governs the search and seizure of digital evidence?
Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012 (RA 10175), the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), and the Rules of Electronic Evidence
3. What is the principle of 'chain of custody' in forensics?
Maintaining Evidence Integrity
4. What does 'informed consent' mean in digital forensic investigations?
Voluntary Agreement
5. What is a key ethical consideration when dealing with personal data?
Privacy
6. Which principle ensures that forensic investigations are not biased?
Objectivity
7. What is the role of a digital forensic expert in court?
Present Evidence
8. What type of data is protected under attorney-client privilege in digital forensics?
Confidential Communication
9. What is one of the challenges in presenting digital forensic evidence in court?
Understanding of Technology
10. What is the most important aspect of ethical conduct for a digital forensics investigator?
Integrity

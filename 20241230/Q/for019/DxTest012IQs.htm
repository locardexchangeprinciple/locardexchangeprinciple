Cyber Threat Intelligence and Incident Response Quiz

1. What is the primary goal of incident response?
Containment and Mitigation
2. Which phase of the incident response process involves identifying the source of an attack?
Identification
3. What is the term used for tracking the actions of a threat actor over time?
Timeline Analysis
4. Which data is crucial for identifying a cyberattack's origin?
Log Data
5. What is the process of gathering and analyzing information about potential threats?
Threat Intelligence
6. Which tool is commonly used for log aggregation and analysis during incident response?
SIEM
7. What is the key difference between active and passive cyber threat intelligence?
Collection Method
8. What is the primary goal of threat hunting?
Proactive Detection
9. What is the first step after discovering a security breach?
Containment
10. Which framework is used to guide incident response and handling processes?
NIST

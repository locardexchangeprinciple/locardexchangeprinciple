Digital Evidence and Cybercrime Basics Quiz

1. What is digital evidence?
Data stored on electronic devices
2. What is 'chain of custody' in digital forensics?
Process of documenting evidence handling
3. What is the primary characteristic of digital evidence?
It exists in digital form and can be stored on electronic devices
4. What is phishing?
Fraudulent attempt to steal sensitive information
5. What type of crime is identity theft?
Cybercrime
6. What is the primary goal of a hacker in a cybercrime?
Accessing unauthorized data
7. What is the first step in handling digital evidence?
Seize and secure the device
8. What is a common form of cybercrime that involves malware?
Ransomware
9. What is the focus of social engineering in cybercrime?
Manipulating individuals into divulging confidential information
10. What is the role of forensic investigators in cybercrime?
Identify, collect, and analyze evidence
